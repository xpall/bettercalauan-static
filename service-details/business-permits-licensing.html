Business

Business Permits & Licensing Office

Business permits, Mayor's clearance, and licensing services

Processing

1-3 Days

Fee

Varies by Business

Who Can Apply

Business Owner

Appointment

Walk-in

Step-by-Step Process

Follow these steps to complete this service

1

Secure Barangay Clearance

Get business clearance from the barangay where your business is located.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather DTI/SEC registration, lease contract, and other requirements.

3

Submit Application

Go to BPLO and submit application with required documents.

4

Pay Fees

Pay business taxes and fees at Treasurer's Office.

5

Claim Permit

Return to BPLO to claim your business permit.

Requirements

Documents needed for your application

New Business

  • DTI/SEC Registration
  • Barangay Business Clearance
  • Contract of Lease or Land Title
  • Fire Safety Certificate
  • Sanitary Permit
  • Valid ID

Renewal

  • Previous Business Permit
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
  • Barangay Business Clearance
  • Updated Fire Safety Certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I renew my business permit?
Before January 20 of each year to avoid penalties.
What are the penalties for late renewal?
A surcharge of 25% plus 2% interest per month on unpaid taxes.
