Process Flow

Flowchart for Legislative Proposal

Step-by-step process for enacting ordinances and resolutions

01

File Proposed Ordinance

Submit proposed ordinance to Sangguniang Bayan for consideration

02

First Reading / Referral to Committee

Initial reading and assignment to relevant committee for review

03

Public Hearing / Committee Action

Committee conducts public hearing and deliberates on proposed ordinance

04

Committee Report

Committee submits findings and recommendations to Sangguniang Bayan

05

Second Reading

Detailed discussion and debate on proposed ordinance

06

Third and Final Reading

Final voting on proposed ordinance by Sangguniang Bayan

07

10-Day Mayor's Approval

Mayor reviews and approves enacted ordinance within 10 days

08

3-Day Submission to SP

Submit approved ordinance to Sangguniang Panlalawigan for review within 3 days

09

SP Review Period

60-day review for appropriation ordinances; 30-day review for others

10

Posting / Publication

Public posting and publication of approved ordinance

11

Implementation

Ordinance takes effect and is enforced within municipality

About

Understanding Local Legislation

Learn about the legislative process of Sangguniang Bayan

Ordinances

Local laws with permanent and general application that require compliance from residents and businesses within the municipality.

Resolutions

Expressions of the legislative body's will or opinion on specific matters, often used for commendations, requests, or policy positions.

Public Participation

Citizens can attend Sangguniang Bayan sessions and participate in public hearings for proposed ordinances.

Transparency

All enacted ordinances and resolutions are made available to the public as part of our commitment to open governance.

Empowering the people of Calauan with transparent access to the services, programs, and public funds of LGU Calauan.
